Our Story

We first met on August 18, 2021, and on August 18, 2024, we are getting married! As fate would have it, it was love at first sight! Our journey together began with a simple 'hello' that blossomed into countless shared dreams, laughter that echoes through our days, and endless support that carries us through life's challenges.

Our relationship is a testament to the power of love, patience, and understanding. In each other, we found not just partners, but best friends who complete each other's sentences and understand the unspoken words. Together, we have built a foundation of trust, respect, and unwavering devotion.

I, the groom, am an engineer born and raised in the heart of Beirut, Lebanon, with Western Armenian roots that have shaped my perspective on family, love, and life. Meanwhile, my lovely Anahit, my bride, hails from the enchanting city of Yerevan, Armenia, bringing with her the warmth of Eastern Armenian heritage.

In the dynamic streets of Yerevan, we found a connection that defies differences. Together, we've discovered the beauty in embracing our distinct backgrounds, learning from one another, and celebrating the richness of our cultures. Our relationship is a tapestry woven with the threads of Lebanese and Armenian traditions, creating a harmonious blend that reflects the love we share.

As an engineer, I see life as a puzzle to be solved, and Anahit, with her commercial prowess, brings the art of negotiation and understanding into our relationship. We complement each other in ways that are both fascinating and inspiring, learning from each other's strengths and supporting one another's dreams.

As we embark on this beautiful journey of marriage, we want to express our heartfelt gratitude to all our friends and family who have supported and cherished our love story. Your presence on our special day will make it even more memorable, and we look forward to celebrating this joyous occasion with you.

With love and excitement,
Anahit & Hrag
